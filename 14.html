Steps to Order Medicine Online from EasyPharmcy.in?


We have both web and mobile presence. This implies you can easily buy medicine online in India sitting in the comforts of your house or on the go! Here is how you do it:
  • For browser, visit EasyPharmcy.in or download the Medlife app from Google Play Store for Android and Appstore for iOS
  • Click on ‘Upload Prescription’
  • If you do not have a prescription, you can consult our doctor
  • Fill up your contact details
  • Place the order
  • You will get a call confirming your medicines
  • Get your medicines delivered at your doorsteps
